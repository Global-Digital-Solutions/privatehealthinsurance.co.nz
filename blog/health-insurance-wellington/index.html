Wellington's health insurance market is strong, with good private hospital facilities and competitive insurance options. Here's what Wellington residents need to know.

Wellington's Private Hospital Network

Wellington has a well-developed private healthcare sector:

- Bowen Hospital (Crofton Downs): Wellington's main private hospital, owned by Southern Cross - Wakefield Hospital (Wellington): General and specialist private hospital - Southern Cross Affiliated Providers: Network of specialist rooms and day surgeries

Health Insurance in Wellington

Wellington residents have access to all major NZ health insurers. Premiums are similar across the country — insurers don't discriminate by region.

Wellington Public Health Waiting Lists

Wellington has significant elective surgery waiting lists, making private insurance particularly valuable: - Orthopaedic procedures: 12–18 months - Ophthalmology: 8–14 months - Gastroenterology: 6–12 months

Local Specialist Access

Wellington has a strong specialist community, particularly in: - Cardiology (Wellington Heart Clinic) - Orthopaedics - Oncology (Wellington Cancer Centre) - Gastroenterology

Private health insurance provides fast, direct access to these specialists.

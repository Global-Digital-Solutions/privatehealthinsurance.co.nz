Why Private Health Insurance in NZ?

New Zealand's public health system is world-class — but waiting lists for planned surgery can stretch months or even years.

⏱️

Skip the Waiting Lists

The average wait for planned surgery in NZ is 4–18 months. With private cover, you can be treated within weeks at a hospital of your choice.

🏥

Private Room & Choice

Choose your own specialist and private hospital. No shared wards — just comfortable, personalised care when you need it most.

💊

Access Non-Pharmac Drugs

Cancer treatments and specialist drugs not funded by Pharmac can cost $50,000+. The right policy covers these life-changing treatments.
